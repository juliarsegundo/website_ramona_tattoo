# 🎨 Ramona Tattoo Landing Page

A landing page developed for a professional tattoo artist, focused on user experience, brand identity, and conversion.

The project was designed to convey delicacy, trust, and exclusivity, guiding visitors through the artist's work, inspiring them with her portfolio, and making it easy to get in touch.

## 🖌️ Design Process

Throughout the development, the main goal was to create a welcoming and unique experience that reflected the tattoo artist's style.

Some of the design principles adopted were:

- minimalist and authentic visual identity;
- a soft color palette chosen by the tattoo artist;
- design tailored to the audience she wanted to attract;
- elegant and readable typography;
- intuitive navigation;
- clear visual hierarchy;
- subtle animations;
- user-centered experience.

---

## 📱 Responsiveness

This project was built following a **Mobile-First** approach, considering that most of the tattoo artist's clients discover her work and make their first contact through their smartphones, mainly via Instagram and WhatsApp.

For this reason, the entire experience was designed with mobile users in mind first, prioritizing usability, performance, and conversion. The layout was then adapted for tablets and desktops, ensuring a consistent experience across different screen sizes.

The project is fully responsive and supports:

- Smartphones
- Tablets
- Desktop devices

## 🧠 Challenges

During development, several challenges required specific solutions:

- optimizing a large number of high-quality tattoo photos without compromising website performance;
- creating a visual identity aligned with the client's artistic style;
- designing a customer journey that reused her existing briefing form before directing users to WhatsApp;
- optimizing the mobile experience;
- configuring a custom domain and HTTPS;
- managing DNS settings and SSL certificate issuance.
